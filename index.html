The Bramacuda

While testing a new experimental diving suit while investigating the Rowsdower Corporation for dumping pollutants into the Pacific Ocean, marine biologist Dr. Eric Bramwell was swept away by a rogue wave and found himself trapped in an underwater cloud of poisonous chemicals. The pollutants reacted violently with the diving suit, fusing the suit to his body and granting him the ability to breathe underwater and communicate with denizens of the sea. Now, doomed to spend eternity below surface, The Bramacuda is determined to take down the Rowsdower Corporation and save the sea once and for all.

CC-422

Prodigy aerospace engineer and test pilot Christopher Anthony was blown to smithereens on the virgin flight of his new experimental supersonic jet. Miraculously, his brain was salvaged from the wreckage and kept alive. Relying on his research and blueprints, the military created an indestructible flight suit under the codename CC-422 and mounted Christopher's brain in the CPU. Now tasked with defending the United States from spy satellites and stealth aircraft, CC-422 is the ultimate weapon in the American arsenal.

The Clawsons

From the pen of Paul Levesque comes The Clawsons, a blend of humor and action set in 1960s Chicago. While celebrating their wedding, Bobby and Elizabeth find themselves trapped in a never-ending acid trip in which they possess fantastic powers. Even their pets, Edna and Louie, are transformed into ferocious beasts hellbent on keeping the couple safe as they traverse the warped, unpredictable version of Chicago in which they find themselves. Will they find their keys? Will their apartment stop rotating? Will this night ever end? Strap in and prepare yourself for this psychedelic journey, but be sure to bring a partner.

T.O. Esquire

After discovering a corruption scandal tying the mayor and most of the city council to money laundering, drug trafficking, and a series of mafia murders, District Attorney Tony Ochs takes the scales of justice into his own hands to clean up the streets of Chicago. He’ll need brains, brawn, and more than a little luck to stay one step ahead of La Cosa Nostra and the mayor’s bloodthirsty henchmen in this gritty, brutal thriller by Rusty Shackleford.

Bobby Stompy

Ransom Casino was the biggest rock band in the world until they agreed to play a private concert at the behest of a shady billionaire. The concert was a setup, leading the bandmates to be kidnapped by a deranged fanatic who performed gruesome scientific experiments on them. Of the four, Bobby Stompy was the sole survivor. Now, he finds himself in a desperate race against time to track down the people behind the experiments before the mutation takes complete control of him and destroys him from the inside out. Delve into this heart-stopping rollercoaster by Armin Tamzarian.

Flea

On the eve of his first title fight, mixed martial artist Aaron McKinnon finds himself in handcuffs, accused of a grisly murder. For a decade, he’s held in isolation at San Quentin Maximum Security Prison for a crime he swears he didn’t commit. Driven to the brink of madness by ten years in isolation, Aaron escapes during a prison riot and disappears into the night. Now, he must stay one step ahead of the authorities and his own insanity as he searches for answers and revenge from the people who stole his freedom. Don’t miss this dark mind-bender by the team of Mark Callous and James Cornette.
